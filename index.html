Life Sometimes Sucks

By Brian Tang




I wake up in the morning from my short-due sleep

As I use the restroom, and then head over to eat

I begrudgingly prepare myself for the day

Because trying to complete life is no easy feat.


It always has a way of bringing you down

Every time you try your best to live

Every good thing comes with some bad

Taking away more than it can give.


Sometimes I wonder why I even bother to try

Considering that in the near future, I'll still die.
